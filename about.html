Who am I? Where do I live? What do I do?

My name is Alex. And I am a practical dreamer. Dreams are abstract and sometimes even difficult to believe they could happen at all. But, I made the decision, not a long time ago, of creating and practicing in real life the events of my favourite dream.

We could say, with certainty, that at the age of 30, there is still an important amount of faith in me, with the addition of an equal important value: perseverance. Despite challenges encountered along the path, I have finally understood that passion comes after repetitive and deliberate hard and deep work. All of this while living in Spain, Italy, United Kingdom and now back to Italy, throughout my life.

What would I like to do in Web Development?

Web development is the medium through which I believe I can embrace my ideas and values and apply them and show them to the world. In other words, web development allows me to show that I try to be another artist among artists.

What are my passions?

My passions are coding, reading - which includes classics, science and sci-fi books - LEGO and robots.

What is my favourite quote?

Ralph Waldo Emerson

But pretty much all of Ralph Waldo Emerson's quotes are worth reading.
